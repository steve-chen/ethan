taroko gorge - Beauty in nature

One of Taiwan's most GORGEous wonders. Have I gotten you there? But seriously, Taroko Gorge is famed for its breathtaking vistas, and I highly recommend it. When I visited there, I was surprised by the beautiful foliage, as well as how it wasn't as hot as the rest of Taiwan, with a gentle wind and refreshing air.

The national park is well known for its lengthy tunnels that run from mountain to mountain. If you're lucky enough, you'll find waterfalls underground! How awesome does that seem? Everlasting streams of water gently carve the tunnels.

The valley covered with luminescent trees has a history that dates back millions of years. The endless streams of water slowly eroded the valley into what it is today. My reccomendation in Taroko gorge national park starts from the Eternal Spring Shrine also called Changchun Shrine. This shrine is a photo jackpot, the backround is scattered with extravagent dark green trees, a shrine holding three Taiwanese tradtional shrines, a waterfall gushing water into the riverbed below.
